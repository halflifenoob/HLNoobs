    How to setup our custom Sven Coop player model pack

    Guide made by Rizapiza

    Step 1: Download our player model pack

    Step 2: Unzip the archive. You should be left with a folder named models, if you don't have this then you messed up.

    Step 3: Locate your Sven Coop directory manually or browse local files via Steam

    Step 4: Copy the models folder over to either svencoop_downloads or svencoop_addons

    (if one gives you issues, delete the models folder from that directory and try again in the other directory)

    Step 5: Join one of the HLNoobs.net Sven Coop Servers, at this point it should be setup so test to see if custom player models are enabled.

    Report any issues to me on our Discord Server, if we can't help then try the Sven Coop Discord server. I refuse to add anything NSFW in the PM pack or my game servers so don't ask or you will likely get banned from our Discord.
