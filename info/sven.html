    Sven Coop

    What is Sven Coop?

    Sven Coop is a standalone cooperative mod for Half-Life, it was originally made by "Sven Viking" aka Daniel Fearons.
    Sven Coop allows for you to play Half-Life and custom community content with other players on a game engine (GoldSrc) that the majority of modern PCs can run.
    Sven Coop was first released in 1999 and is 24 years old.
